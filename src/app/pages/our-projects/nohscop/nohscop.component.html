Project Description.

Nohscope | Mepa Monitoring System is transforming the way Sri Lanka protects its marine environment from ship-related damage. Currently, monitoring vessel activity, tracking potential threats, and responding to environmental risks is a fragmented and reactive process, often resulting in delayed actions and missed opportunities for swift intervention. Traditional methods of monitoring are time-consuming, prone to errors, and lack real-time visibility, which hinders the effectiveness of maritime protection efforts.

By digitizing and automating the entire monitoring process, Nohscope provides a seamless, real-time solution for tracking ships, detecting potential environmental hazards, and ensuring rapid response to prevent damage.With Nohscope, maritime authorities can make proactive decisions, minimizing risks to Sri Lanka’s vital marine ecosystems and ensuring a safer, more sustainable environment for generations to come.
